Unit 1 Summary

Unit 1 covered basic computer science concepts like binary numbers, digital data, and programming fundamentals. We learned how computers use binary numbers to store and process information, and the difference between lossy and lossless data compression. Intellectual property rights were also introduced, emphasizing the importance of respecting creators' work. Through practical activities, we gained a clear understanding of these core concepts, laying a strong foundation for further learning in computer science.
